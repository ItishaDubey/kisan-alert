WhatsApp-based agricultural advisory for small and marginal farmers — crop advice, weather alerts, and disease diagnosis in their own language.

What it does
Send a text, voice note, or photo. Get a reply in under 4 seconds — in Hindi, Kannada, Marathi, or Telugu.
🌱

Crop Recommendation

Satellite NDVI + soil moisture data (Google Earth Engine) combined with Gemini gives pre-sowing crop advice specific to your district and season.

🌦️

Dry-Spell Alerts

Hyperlocal weather data (Open-Meteo) checked every morning. If dry-spell risk crosses a threshold, farmers get a proactive WhatsApp alert — no need to ask.

🔍

Crop Health + RSK Follow-up

Send a photo or voice note of a sick crop. Gemini Vision diagnoses it, logs the case, and notifies the nearest Rythu Seva Kendra for expert follow-up.
